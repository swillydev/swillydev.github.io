Road Traffic Law Services

The reality is that the decisions we make while driving can have serious consequences. Getting the right legal advice at the right time can significantly reduce the impact these decisions may have on you and your family.

When you first contact us, we will listen to your concerns and outline how we can tailor our fees to meet both your expectations and budget. Whether you're facing a notice of intended prosecution, summons, or arrest, we are here to guide you through every step of the court process. We can also provide legal advice when needed, allowing you to choose the level of support that best fits your needs. Our approach is flexible and personalized to ensure you get the help you need.

Types of Motoring Law Cases We Handle:

  • Taxi Touting
  • Mobile phone offences
  • Drink driving offences
  • Drug driving offences
  • Speeding tickets
  • Failure to provide a specimen
  • Twelve points – totting up
  • Driving while disqualified
  • Early removal of a driving disqualification
  • Motoring offence appeals
  • Driving without insurance
  • Road rage incidents
  • Dangerous driving
  • Special reasons for not disqualifying
  • Endorsement and penalty points
  • Causing death by driving

What Do We Offer:

Our expert driving offence solicitors provide clear, concise, and practical legal advice, no matter how complex the situation may seem. From drink driving to driving while disqualified or even causing death by driving, we specialize in motoring offences and offer straightforward advice free from legal jargon.

While we are based in Sheffield, our clients come from all over England and Wales, thanks to our strong network of legal professionals and expert witnesses. This allows us to offer national coverage and ensure you're receiving the best possible advice.

Although many driving offences are non-imprisonable and do not qualify for Legal Aid, they can still result in the loss of your driving licence, which can have far-reaching consequences. At Haleys Solicitors, we offer reasonable pricing and, in many cases, fixed fees, depending on the specifics of your case.

At Haleys Solicitors, we understand how important your driving license is to your life and livelihood. Our dedicated team will work tirelessly to protect your interests and achieve the best possible outcome in your case.
