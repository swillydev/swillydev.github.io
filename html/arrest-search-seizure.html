Arrest, Search and Seizure Services

Expert Legal Support for Police Powers

A person's arrest, the search of their premises and the seizure of their property can be hugely damaging. Our specialized team provides expert guidance and representation in cases involving police powers and their proper use.

If you've been arrested, it's likely that your home or business premises have been searched, and your property has been seized. The consequences of this can be devastating—leading to significant disruption, financial loss, and long-term damage to your reputation.

Our Expertise: Defending Your Rights

At Haleys Solicitors, we specialize in protecting your rights during search and seizure operations, as well as defending against unlawful arrests. We understand how challenging it can be when state authorities infringe upon your personal and business assets. Our expert team is one of the few in the UK with the specialized knowledge needed to effectively challenge the legality of these actions.

Searches are often conducted under search warrants or powers triggered by an arrest. These can lead to the seizure of critical items like mobile phones, computers, and sensitive documents, which could have serious implications for your business and personal life.

Authorities sometimes carry out unlawful arrests or use flawed reasons to seek search warrants. If this happens, we provide immediate legal action to challenge these abuses of power. Our team will work quickly to assess whether your arrest or search was unlawful and guide you on the best course of action.

We offer swift and effective legal advice to ensure your rights are protected, and we will challenge the misuse of power by state agents. Don’t wait—if you’ve been subjected to an unlawful arrest or search, let Haleys Solicitors help you get the justice and compensation you deserve.
