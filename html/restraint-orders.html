Restraint and Freezing Orders

Expert Legal Support for Restraint Orders

The power of the state to restrain or freeze assets has drastically increased in recent years. At Haleys Solicitors, we understand the significant impact these orders can have on individuals and businesses. Our expert team specializes in challenging and managing restraint and freezing orders effectively.

Our Expertise

The impact of asset freezing or restraint orders on an individual or business can be devastating. These orders are often sought by authorities with the knowledge that they can have severe consequences. In many cases, the purpose behind these orders is to disrupt financial stability, and authorities may intentionally use them as a tool for financial leverage.

Account Freezing Orders are frequently used by law enforcement because they are easier to obtain, and the authorities are financially incentivized to seek forfeiture without the time-consuming and costly process of pursuing a criminal case through conviction in the Crown Court. Unfortunately, these powers can be misused, and they are vulnerable to challenge. If successful, challenging these orders can lead to compensation and coverage of legal costs.

Similarly, Civil Recovery Proceedings may be initiated when a criminal prosecution has either failed or was never started. Restraint Orders are typically imposed on individuals or businesses under criminal investigation, and their consequences can be equally damaging.

The potential damage caused by these orders is far-reaching. However, a successful challenge can significantly alter the course of the case and provide a substantial setback to the investigating authorities. Unfortunately, many individuals are advised that challenging these orders is not possible or that it is best to wait until a later, unspecified time to act.

At Haleys Solicitors, we take a proactive approach in defending our clients' rights. Our experience shows that challenging these severe orders early in the case can often provide a significant advantage, and we are committed to using every tool at our disposal to protect your interests. With our expert legal guidance, we’ll work to ensure that you aren’t left vulnerable to unnecessary financial harm.
