Criminal Law Services

Expert Criminal Law Representation

At Haleys Solicitors, our criminal law specialists have built an outstanding reputation for representing individuals charged with serious crimes. With years of experience and in-depth knowledge of the legal system, we are true experts in criminal law, ensuring that you receive the highest level of representation.

Whether facing a minor or high-profile case, our team is dedicated to providing exceptional legal support, from the initial consultation through to the resolution of your case. We understand the stakes involved and are committed to fighting for the best possible outcome on your behalf.

Our highly skilled criminal defence team offers expert legal support, guidance, and representation for a wide range of charges, including:

  • Conspiracy Offences
  • Corporate Manslaughter
  • Criminal Damage
  • Terrorism
  • Murder & Manslaughter
  • Fraud & Regulatory Law
  • Human Trafficking
  • GBH, ABH & Assault
  • Sexual Offences
  • Proceeds of Crime

  • Extradition
  • Robbery, Theft & Burglary
  • Public Order Offences
